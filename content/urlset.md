Each site has a URLs page (in the site sidebar) for controlling exactly which pages get scanned.

## Active

The pages currently being scanned. Search, select, and remove URLs you don't want scanned. Removed URLs are excluded from your site's score.

Reset from sitemap re-syncs your page list against your site's current sitemap: new sitemap pages are added, pages no longer in the sitemap are retired, and pages back in the sitemap are reactivated.

## Add URLs

Add pages one per line, or upload a `.txt` file. Only URLs on the site's own domain are accepted.

## Redirects

Pages that redirect elsewhere. These are tracked separately and excluded from your site's score.

## Retired

Pages that are no longer available (removed manually, or dropped from the sitemap). Also excluded from your site's score.
