## Subscribing

Subscribe to a paid plan at [scangov.com/plans](https://scangov.com/plans). After checkout, ScanGov automatically provisions your account and kicks off your first full-site scan.

## Changing plans

Already on a paid plan? Go to Org > Plans to see your current plan and switch to a different tier. Plan changes take effect immediately — no new checkout required.

## Canceling

Cancel from the same Org > Plans page, which links to the billing portal for managing or canceling your subscription.
