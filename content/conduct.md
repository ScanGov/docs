ScanGov's community — contributors, maintainers, and anyone participating in our GitHub repos, Discord, or other community spaces — is expected to communicate respectfully and in good faith. Harassment, personal attacks, and other unprofessional conduct aren't tolerated. Community leaders may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove anyone who violates these standards.

Our code of conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct.html), the same standard used by many open source projects. To report a concern, [contact us](/contact).
