## Starting an account

* [Sign up](https://scangov.com/plans) for a [ScanGov plan](https://scangov.com/plans) to create an account
* [Log in](https://my.scangov.com) to your ScanGov account

Based on your role, you can:

* Configure your [organization](https://my.scangov.com/organization)
* Add [sites](https://my.scangov.com/scan)
* Manage [domains](https://my.scangov.com/domainconfig)
* Set up [single sign-on](https://my.scangov.com/sso-config)
* Invite [people](https://my.scangov.com/members)
* Create [filters](https://my.scangov.com/listfilters)
* Manage your [plan](/plans)

## Roles

ScanGov account types:

* Admin: Can add, remove and manage other accounts and sites
* Member: Can view and use ScanGov

## Manage people

1. [Log in](https://my.scangov.com) to your ScanGov account
2. Click [Org](https://my.scangov.com/organization) in the top navigation bar
3. Click [People](https://my.scangov.com/members) in the sidebar
   * Change roles or remove people
   * Invite new people

## Inviting someone

From People, click Add new person, enter their email and choose a role (Admin or Member), then send. They'll get an email with a link to join your organization.

## Organization settings

Org > Settings lets admins rename the organization.
